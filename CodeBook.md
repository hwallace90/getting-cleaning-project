"subject"
    
    The number of the volunteer the data comes from
       
        1-30
        
"actName"

    The name of the activity performed by the subject
    
        WALKING
        WALKING_UPSTAIRS
        WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
        SITTING
        STANDING
        LAYING

"test.train"

    Whether the subject was part of the test or training data
        test
        train
